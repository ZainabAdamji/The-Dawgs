Consider adopting a dog

Only 1 in 10 dogs born will find a permanent home. Shelters euthanize many animals due to a lack of space and adopters. Adopting a shelter dog can give them a second chance at a happy life. Shelter dogs often show gratitude and loyalty to their new families.
